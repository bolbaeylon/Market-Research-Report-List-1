<p><p>The radiation monitoring and safety market encompasses various detector types, each serving specific applications. Gas-filled detectors, like Geiger-Muller counters, measure ionizing radiation through gas ionization. Scintillators use luminescent materials to detect radiation by producing light when interacting with charged particles. Semiconductor-based detectors utilize materials like silicon to convert radiation into electrical signals, offering high precision. Additionally, "Others" may include dosimeters and personal radiation monitors. Together, these technologies ensure effective radiation detection and safety across industries.</p></p>
