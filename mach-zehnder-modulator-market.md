<p><p>A Mach Zehnder Modulator (MZM) is an optical device used to modulate light signals in communication systems. It operates by splitting a light beam into two paths, where one path experiences a phase shift based on an applied voltage. The phase-modulated beams then recombine, resulting in changes to the output intensity. MZMs are essential in high-speed fiber optic communications, serving applications in telecommunications, data centers, and increasingly in advanced technologies like quantum computing.</p><p>The Mach Zehnder Modulator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
